# MeSH Neoplasms (C04) Hierarchy Summary

## Overview

The MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) tree organizes neoplasms under code **C04**. The hierarchy has 7 levels, with more specific cancer types at deeper levels.

## Key Observations

1. **Common cancers are at Level 5-6**: Lung, Prostate, Colorectal, Ovarian, Kidney, Bladder are all at level 5 or 6.

2. **Blood cancers are different**: Leukemias and Lymphomas are under "Histologic Type" (C04.557) not "By Site" (C04.588).

3. **Breast cancer is Level 3**: Unusually, Breast Neoplasms (D001943) is at level 3, making it a broader category.

4. **For ~60 specific cancers**: Focus on **Level 5** (37 terms) + some Level 6 (5 terms) + Level 4 blood cancers.
